The Women Of Hip-Hop

Here are female artists where we bring awarness to female artisits who don't get the recognition they should be accredited for.

Beyonce

Beyonce was first recognized when she was the lead singer of her childhood group, Destiny’s Child. She started her individual career releasing her first album Dangerously in Love, and with that, became one of music’s top selling artist Her no.1 hits were Perfect, Check on It, Independent Women, Baby Boy, Crazy in Love, Bills, Bills Bills, Bootylicious, Single Ladies, Say My Name, and Irreplaceable. Click On her Name!

Cardi B

Cardi got her attention through the media initially as a stripper. She started off appearing on Love & His Hop: New York in 2015 leaving in 2017 to proceed at starting music full time. She then released her no.1 single Bodak Yellow in 2017 Her no.1 hits were Bodak Yellow, and I Like It. Click On her Name!

Rihanna

Rihanna started her career when she was only 16, signing with Def Jam records and releasing her first Album in 2005 Her no.1 hits were Umbrella, SOS, Take a Bow, Only Girl, Diamonds, and Work. Click On her Name!
