The Facts About Female Artists In The Music Industry

by Jasmin Stewart

"women have been absent from history — that absence matters — therefore women must be restored to history."

Jill Mathews

The leading global think tank studied issues of inclusion across entertainment mediums, and has set its sights on the music world. The USC Annenberg School for Communication and Journalism division just released its inaugural report on inclusion in popular music and the results reveal that just like in the film and television industry, there is a strong need for more females in the music industry.
