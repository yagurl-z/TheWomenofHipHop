About Us

by Celeste Giddens

Hello. Our site is called The Women of Hip Hop. Our goal and mission is to show awareness, and help female artist in the industry. Females are under represented in the music industry, from rappers to producers. Our Website has a few different components in it. We give you examples of successful females in the industry along with facts and statistics. We also give you tips on how to get your music out there and a few words of encouragment. Last we will give you facts on why women are less represented.

"My tears have dried and I know that no weapon formed against me will prosper"

Nicki Minaj
We hope you enjoy our website and that it encourages you to pursue your dreams no matter how the statistics look nor if people believe you can do it.
